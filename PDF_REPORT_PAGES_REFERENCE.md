## 📐 Dimensions & Specifications

### Page Size
- Format: Letter (8.5" × 11")
- Margins: 0.5" on all sides
- Usable area: 7.5" × 10"

### Chart Sizes
- Full-width charts: 6.5" × 4"
- Stat boxes: 6.5" × 2"
- Donut chart: 4" × 4"

### Font Specifications
| Element | Font | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| Page Title | Helvetica | 28pt | Bold |
| Section Heading | Helvetica | 16pt | Bold |
| Body Text | Helvetica | 10-11pt | Regular |
| Table Header | Helvetica | 9pt | Bold |
| Table Data | Helvetica | 8pt | Regular |
| Footer | Helvetica | 8pt | Italic |

### Resolution
- Chart DPI: 300 (print quality)
- Image Format: PNG
- Color Mode: RGB

## 🎨 Styling Guidelines

### Header Section (Page 1)
- Deep Navy background OR white background with navy text
- Large bold title: "PredictMed Healthcare Recovery Forecast"
- Hospital name and date in Teal
- Clear visual hierarchy

### Stat Boxes
- White background with Teal accent border
- Navy text for values
- Trend indicators (+ green, - red)
- Proper padding and spacing

### Charts
- White background (always)
- Navy axes and labels
- Teal primary bars/lines
- Navy grid lines (30% opacity)
- Black borders (1-2pt)
- Professional title with 15pt padding

### Tables
- Navy header (with white text)
- Alternating row colors
- 1pt navy grid lines
- Centered alignment (default)
- Proper padding (3-4pt)

### Color Consistency
- All sections use same color palette
- No gradients or complex effects
- Solid colors only
- High contrast for readability

## 🖨️ Print Recommendations

### Paper
- Type: Bright white 20lb bond
- Size: 8.5" × 11" (Letter)
- Quality: Premium for best results

### Printer Settings
- Color: Full color (not grayscale)
- Quality: Best/High
- Scaling: 100% (no scaling)
- Orientation: Portrait
- Margins: None (use printer defaults)

- First page: Clear title and stats
